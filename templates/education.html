About Me

Hi, I’m a Data Analyst passionate about turning data into actionable insights that drive real results. Over time, I’ve built a solid track record working on data-driven projects that involve everything from analysis and visualization to model development and business strategy support.
My journey into data science started with a postgraduate program in Data Science, and since then, I’ve continued to refine my skills through real-world projects and continuous learning across multiple platforms. I enjoy using tools like Python, SQL, and machine learning to uncover insights, solve complex problems, and help teams make smarter, evidence-based decisions.
What excites me most is seeing how data can transform ideas into measurable impact—whether it’s optimizing operations, improving customer experiences, or guiding strategic decisions. I’m always eager to take on new challenges, collaborate with innovative teams, and keep growing as a data professional.
If you’re looking for someone who’s analytical, creative, and driven to make a real difference through data, I’d love to connect and explore how I can add value to your organization. Let’s turn data into results!
